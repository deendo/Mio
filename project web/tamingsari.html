MENARA TAMING SARI

Attractions in Malacca City

Visit one of Melaka's popular and iconic landmark, the Menara Taming Sari. This tower allows visitors to see a panoramic, 360 degree view of Melaka City, St Paul's hill, the Straits of Melaka and more. Feel a rush of thrill and excitement as you are launched upwards at a height of 80 meters off the ground. Get your camera ready and take amazing photos while you are high up in the sky.

Book your tickets now and bring your whole family to this iconic landmark.
